Overview

Who the R Series is for

The R Series is where the lineup turns serious about both luxury and performance. It's one of the most customizable pontoons Bennington builds, with an extensive catalog of luxury floorplans and features. Buyers who want the boat built around exactly how they entertain, cruise, and play end up here.

Dynamic performance is the other half of the story: the R is built to move, not just lounge. It's the pick for owners who host big crews on big water and refuse to choose between comfort and capability.
