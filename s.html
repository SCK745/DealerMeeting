Overview

Who the S Series is for

The S Series is Bennington's "have it your way" line, built for buyers who want a personal boat at an accessible price. It carries Bennington's best-selling floorplans and a wide range of upgrades and colors, so an S rarely leaves the dealership looking like any other S.

Redesigned for 2027, it now borrows helm equipment that used to live tiers above it. If you want a real Bennington you can make your own without stepping into the premium tiers, start here.
