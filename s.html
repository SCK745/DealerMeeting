Overview

Who the S Series is for

The S Series is Bennington's entry point — the "have it your way" line built for first-time pontoon owners and value-focused families who still want a boat that feels personal. It carries Bennington's best-selling floorplans and a wide range of upgrades and colors, so an S rarely leaves the dealership looking like any other S.

Redesigned for 2026, it now borrows helm equipment that used to live tiers above it. If you want the lowest-cost path to a real Bennington without giving up customization, start here.
