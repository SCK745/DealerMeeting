Overview

Who the Q Series is for

The Q Series is Bennington's premium tier, sitting directly below the flagship QX. It pairs genuine luxury amenities with real performance. This is the boat for owners who want top-shelf materials, a refined helm, and confident open-water manners without going all the way to the flagship.

Think of it as the QX's philosophy at a more approachable price: refined cruising first, with the equipment and finish to make every outing feel like an occasion.
