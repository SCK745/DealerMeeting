Overview

Who the RX Series is for

The RX Sport sits above the R as the lineup's performance-oriented tier. Its athletic silhouette makes the intent obvious from the dock, and the sport-tuned running gear backs it up on the water. This is the Bennington for owners who tow riders, run bigger water, and want the boat to feel eager rather than stately.

Crucially, the sport focus doesn't strip out comfort — plush seating and premium finishes carry over, so the crew rides in luxury while the helm gets to play.
