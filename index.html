Where to Start

Four questions that pick your series.

Shopping on value?

Start at the S One, the simplest and most affordable Bennington, or the S Series for the "have it your way" line, redesigned for 2027.

Want more style and equipment?

The LX Series adds a sleeker look above the S, the LT Series refines it a step further, and the M Series brings more standard equipment and exclusive layouts.

Customization or sport?

The R Series is one of the most customizable pontoons on the water, the RT opens up the bow for swim-ready versatility, and the RX is the sport-tuned athlete of the family.

Chasing top-shelf luxury?

The Q Series delivers premium amenities one step below the top. The QX is the flagship: carbon fiber, Commander Dash 2.0, up to 1,000 HP.
