Where to Start

Three questions that pick your rung.

Shopping on value?

Start at the S Series — Bennington's "have it your way" entry line, redesigned for 2026 — or step to the M Series for more standard equipment and layouts.

Want customization or sport?

The R Series is one of the most customizable pontoons on the water. Want it athletic and sport-tuned? That's the RX.

Chasing top-shelf luxury?

The Q Series delivers premium amenities one step below the top. The all-new 2026 QX is the flagship — carbon fiber, Commander Dash 2.0, up to 1,000 HP.
