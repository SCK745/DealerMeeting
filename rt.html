Who the RT Series is for

The RT slots between the R and the RX with a layout the rest of the lineup doesn't offer: an open bow that turns the front of the boat into a social, swim-ready space. It pairs that runabout-style versatility with the performance and finish of the R family.

It's the pick for crews who split their day between cruising, swimming, and watersports, and want a performance boat that switches between them without compromise.
