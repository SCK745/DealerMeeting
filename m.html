Overview

Who the M Series is for

The M Series is the natural step up from the S and LX, often called the soul of Bennington's lineup. It's for buyers who want more boat without jumping into the luxury tiers: more standard equipment, more floorplan choices, and layouts the S simply can't reach.

If the S is the value pick and the R is the customization playground, the M is the sweet spot in between, the series most families land on when they plan to keep the boat for years.
